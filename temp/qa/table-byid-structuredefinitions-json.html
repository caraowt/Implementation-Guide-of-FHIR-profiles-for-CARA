Observation

An observation is used to track the status of the patient on a given goal.

AllergyIntolerance

This profile is used to track the allergies and intolerances of a patient based on a given value set.

Advance Directives in EHR

The anticipate directives are in the EHR of the patient. This field is present and is a boolean

Existence of Advance Directives

This field is used to indicate whether a patient has advanced directives

Advance Directives Location

This field is used to indicate where the advance directives are if they exist and are not located in the EHR.

Attention Point

Attention Point on the observation

Patient

This profile extension is used to ensure the fields of the master patient index are properly used throughout the implementation.

Care Team

The care team is comprised of the patient, his legal representatives, healthcare professionals contributing to his shared care plan and externals.

Care Plan

A Care Plan tailored to the needs of CARA with advanced directives

Comment

Used to describe the social background of a patient affiliated to CARA

Condition

A condition is used too describe the clinical status and diagnosis made to a patient.

Country of Birth

This field is used to render the country of birth of the patient

Goal

The purpose of a Goal is to track a topic around the Shared Care Plan with its associated list of goals.

Historical Data

Used to describe the historical background of a patient affiliated to CARA

Organization

This profile is used to add organizations to a care team.

Patient Priorities

Used to describe the priorities of a patient with regard to his SCP

Related Person

This profile is used to add externals to a care team.

Show note in SCP monitoring screen

Notes are by default not shown in the SCP monitoring screen. However a member of the SCP interprofessional team can choose to disclose it to the rest of the team.

Social Data

Used to describe the social background of a patient affiliated to CARA
